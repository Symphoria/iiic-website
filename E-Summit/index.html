Speakers

Prof. Vinayak Nath

CEO of Venture Catalysts

Professor Vinayak Nath is the founder and CEO of Venture Catalysts, India’s first integrated incubator. An alumnus of XLRI Jamshedpur, he was the Member Expert at Planning Commission (India), UP for a study group on Higher Education Integration with Vocational Education.

As Founder and MD of Naukri Mahakumbh, he started an organisation with a vision of generating continuous influx of jobs in U.P. His team organised several Job Fairs for the youth of Uttar Pradesh. More than 5000 youths (3738 from Uttar Pradesh) became employed and more than 10000 youth got career counselling.

He will be the Keynote Speaker for the e-Summit to be held during Aparoksha, IIIT Allahabad. He will also be a judge for the Business Plan contest.

Dharamveer Chouhan

Co-founder, Zostel and Zo Rooms

Dharamveer Chouhan – the name which has become a hot topic amongst the investors & tech-business community in the recent times, is the proud founder of Zostel.com & Zo Rooms.

Zostel is a chain of hostels spread across India which has been started to offer budgeted yet luxurious – air-conditioned dormitories, both mixed and female-only to backpackers from across the world. It aims to provide safe, clean and cheap accommodation to budget travellers, especially in the 18-35 age groups. Whereas on the other end, Zo Rooms simply put is a low-budget hotel chain that enables the masses to book rooms (be it at 2am) through a mobile-app, which geo-detects a location and books into the nearest available Zo Room.

Sandro Stephen

Regional Head (North India) | Indian Angel Network

Sandro Stephen is the Regional Head, North India Operations for the Indian Angel Network. He has been associated with the Indian startup space for over 5 years. He has worked with organising various events such as investor pitch sessions, helping entrepreneurs articulate their vision and value proposition, network, deal sourcing - thereby helping to foster the spirit of entrepreneurship across the country.

He primarily works with expanding outreach and community building, including expansion to tier 2 and tier 3 cities in India. He will be speaking on how the startup scene has boomed over the years in India, and what future prospects and concerns the unique proposition of startups in India represent.

This talk will be extremely beneficial for all who wish to know the inner workings of Indian startups, current industry trends and the skills in demand today.

Zunaid Ahmed

CEO of Knudge.me

He is an Alumnus of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad. He instilled the Startup Culture in the institute and inspired many students to start a startup journey of their own. He is an inspiration for all aspiring student startups.
